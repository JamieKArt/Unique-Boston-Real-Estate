Meet Josh

Josh Konoff is a seasoned real estate specialist with more than 8 years of experience. He is dedicated to building long-term relationships with clients and their referrals.  He has lived in the Allston-Brookline-Brighton area for 16 years.

Josh is also the entrepreneur and Executive Director of the Boston Volunteers, one of Boston’s fastest growing nonprofits.
